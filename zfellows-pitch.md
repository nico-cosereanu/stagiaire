# Z Fellows Loom Pitch — Stagiaire

**Length:** ~3 min
**Audience:** Baylor Adams, Z Fellows
**Ask:** $10k

---

## [0:00–0:30] Open — webcam

> Hey Baylor, it's Nico — long time no see. Just got back from filming MasterChef, had some free time before I graduate, so I built Airbnb for chef internships. They're called stages.
>
> You've probably seen those videos on TikTok and Insta — people going to work at fine dining restaurants for a few days. That's a stage. They can be a few days, a few months, sometimes years.

## [0:30–1:00] Problem — webcam

> The problem is they're hard to get if you don't know where to look. They're annoying to schedule. Chefs have horrible hours and restaurants are notoriously bad at responding to email.
>
> A lot of people have asked me how I got my stage at the Michelin-starred spot in Belgium. I figured — maybe there's something there.

## [1:00–1:15] Transition to demo — start screen share

> Let me quickly walk you through it, it's pretty intuitive.

## [1:15–2:15] Walkthrough

Hit these beats while clicking around — don't script the words, just remember the order:

- `/discover` → "660 Michelin restaurants in France, filterable by city, cuisine, stars, dates"
- Click into a restaurant (Septime is a good one) → show the profile
- Show the request flow → "two clicks, the chef on the other end gets it in a clean inbox"
- Mention messaging + reviews briefly → "and after the stage they review each other, symmetric reveal like Airbnb"

## [2:15–2:45] The pitch — back to webcam

> Pitch is — if there's anyone I trust taking money from, it's you guys at Z Fellows. $10k would help get this in front of people.
>
> Products nowadays are kind of useless to pitch — anyone can execute with a Claude Max plan. What's not easy is distribution, and that's where I think I have an edge.
>
> I'm in the industry. I'm working for a billionaire this summer as their private chef. I'm on the path to becoming an influencer off the back of MasterChef. I've worked in a Michelin kitchen. And I have the drive to put this in as many faces as possible.

## [2:45–3:00] The plan + close — webcam, look into camera

> $10k goes to three things — pushing through culinary institutes, organic content, and email campaigns to restaurants. That's it.
>
> Let me know what you think. Talk soon.

---

## Delivery notes

- **Don't read this.** Internalize the beats and let it come out in your voice. Looms feel canned the second they sound rehearsed.
- **Energy > polish.** One take, mistakes in, ship it. Baylor is buying you, not the script.
- **Show the product live** — actually click around in real time. Don't use a recorded demo. It signals "this is real."
- **Anchor him on 660.** That's the wow moment. Make sure your screen is on `/discover` when you say it.
- **Pause after** "if there's anyone I trust taking money from, it's you guys." Half a second of silence. The line lands harder with a beat behind it.
- **Title the Loom** something like "Stagiaire — Nico — Z Fellows" so it's easy for him to find later.
